Our menu

Chicken

Chicken is one of the most versatile and popular dishes in the world. It can be prepared in many ways: bake, fry, stew or boil. Chicken goes well with various spices and marinades, making each dish unique.

Beef

Beef is a meat with a rich taste, which is appreciated for its texture and rich aroma. It is used to cook a variety of dishes, from steaks and burgers to stews and goulash.

Sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish that includes rice, fish and other ingredients such as vegetables or seaweed. They can be in different forms: rolls, nigiri or sashimi, and are often served with soy sauce, wasabi and pickled ginger.
